Rice Pudding

Description

Rice pudding is a creamy and comforting dessert made from rice, milk, sugar, and flavored with vanilla or cinnamon. It's a popular treat enjoyed around the world.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup white rice
  • 4 cups milk
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon (optional)
  • Raisins or nuts for garnish (optional)

Instructions

  1. Rinse the rice under cold water until the water runs clear.
  2. In a large saucepan, combine the rice and 2 cups of water. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat to low, cover, and simmer for about 15 minutes or until the water is absorbed.
  3. Add the milk and sugar to the cooked rice. Stir well to combine.
  4. Cook over medium heat, stirring frequently, until the mixture thickens and the rice is tender, about 20-25 minutes.
  5. Remove from heat and stir in the vanilla extract. If desired, sprinkle with ground cinnamon.
  6. Let the rice pudding cool slightly before serving. Garnish with raisins or nuts if desired.
  7. Serve warm or chilled.
