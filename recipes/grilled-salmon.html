Grilled Salmon

Description

Grilled Salmon is a delicious and healthy main dish featuring fresh salmon fillets seasoned with herbs and spices, then grilled to perfection. It's perfect for a quick and flavorful meal.

Ingredients

  • 2 salmon fillets
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh lemon wedges for serving

Instructions

  1. Preheat the grill to medium-high heat.
  2. Brush the salmon fillets with olive oil on both sides.
  3. In a small bowl, mix garlic powder, paprika, salt, and pepper.
  4. Sprinkle the seasoning mixture evenly over both sides of the salmon fillets.
  5. Place the salmon fillets on the grill, skin-side down.
  6. Grill for about 4-5 minutes per side, or until the salmon is cooked through and flakes easily with a fork.
  7. Remove the salmon from the grill and let it rest for a few minutes.
  8. Serve the grilled salmon with fresh lemon wedges.
