Spaghetti Bolognese

Description

Spaghetti Bolognese is a classic Italian pasta dish featuring a rich and savory meat sauce served over al dente spaghetti. It's a hearty and comforting meal perfect for any occasion.

Ingredients

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 400g ground beef
  • 400g canned tomatoes
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh basil leaves for garnish

Instructions

  1. Cook the spaghetti according to package instructions until al dente. Drain and set aside.
  2. Heat olive oil in a large pan over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and minced garlic, saut√© until softened.
  3. Add the ground beef to the pan and cook until browned.
  4. Stir in the canned tomatoes, tomato paste, and dried oregano. Season with salt and pepper.
  5. >Reduce heat and let the sauce simmer for 20-30 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  6. Serve the sauce over the cooked spaghetti and garnish with fresh basil leaves.
